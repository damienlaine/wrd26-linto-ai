About World Radio Day

A UNESCO Initiative

Proclaimed in 2011 by UNESCO Member States and formally adopted by the UN General Assembly in 2012, World Radio Day celebrates broadcasters worldwide.

2026 Theme: AI & Radio

When used ethically and responsibly to support professional judgement and creativity, AI can strengthen audience trust in radio broadcasting.

Global Collaboration

Radio stations are encouraged to connect internationally, exchanging programs, guests, and sound archives to foster collaboration beyond borders.
